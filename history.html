The history of our company

John Webbis, a passionate entrepreneur with a deep love for the holiday season, embarked on a remarkable journey that led to the creation of Warber, a company dedicated to selling exquisite Christmas ornaments through its online platform.


The journey was not without its challenges. John spent countless hours researching and sourcing the finest Christmas ornaments, each chosen for its exceptional quality and unique design. He collaborated with artisans and craftsmen from different countries, fostering relationships built on trust and a shared love for the holiday spirit.


Today, Warber continues to thrive, offering an extensive collection of Christmas ornaments that delight customers worldwide. John Webbis's vision has evolved into a beloved brand, synonymous with the joy and magic of the holiday season. As the company looks to the future, it remains dedicated to spreading warmth, happiness, and the spirit of Christmas to homes around the globe, one beautifully crafted ornament at a time.
