About us

At Warber, we believe that the magic of Christmas should be celebrated year-round. Founded by John Webbis, an avid Christmas enthusiast, our company is on a mission to bring the spirit of the holiday season to your doorstep, no matter the time of year.


With a deep-rooted passion for Christmas and an unwavering commitment to quality, we've curated a collection of exquisite Christmas ornaments that capture the essence of this cherished holiday. From traditional and timeless designs to modern and whimsical creations, our ornaments are a testament to the craftsmanship and artistry of artisans worldwide. We take pride in offering you a carefully selected range of ornaments that not only adorn your home but also fill it with warmth and joy.
